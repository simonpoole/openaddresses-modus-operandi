Copyright does not enter the picture at this level.

Access to Government geo-data is regulated in the federal and cantonal geo-information laws. To determine on what terms a dataset can be distributed you need to check the relevant meta-data for special conditions and if none, the relevant terms of use or similar published by the data owner. Assuming that just because you can access a server or a dataset and can't immediately find T&Cs for it naturally does not mean it is a free for all (and wouldn't with copyright either).
